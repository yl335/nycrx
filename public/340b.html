Get the Most out of 340B at Every Step of the Way

Medical costs increased by 6.9% between 2009 and 2010*. In 2010, 629,000 people in New York reported being unable to get needed medical care. In the same year, 1,028,000 people reported being uninsured.
(Source: DOH Epi Query 2010)

340B Drug Pricing Program was created by HRSA and OFA to offer meaningful savings to patients on their medications (average 50% off), and to generate significant revenue to the safety net providers (entities). 340B is available to all FQHCs, DSH Hospitals, FQHC look-alikes as well as several other types of providers.
Check if your organization is eligible.

However, the 340B program is currently severely underutilized. Many safety net providers eligible for 340B aren't taking advantage of the program. Some of the reasons for not pursuing the 340B opportunity are the lack of outpatient pharmacy relationship, and perceived complexity of the program.

This is Why NYCRx was Created

From selecting partnering pharmacies, contracting, handling of all Federal paperwork, to ongoing management of 340B transactions, reporting, and auditing once the program is live.
