Research

NYCRx collaborates on projects that further recognition of the importance of prescription access.

SBHC Reproductive Health

Using 340B to help to reduce teenage pregnancy in NYC.

Differential Drug Pricing in NYC

Determining if prescription access is affected by location and neighborhood factors.

Medication Adherence in Type II Diabetes Patients

Studying methods to improve medication adherence in patients with Type II Diabetes.

New York State Medicaid “Carve-in” Study

Understanding the impact of Medicaid Managed Care inclusion in 340B at clinic level.
