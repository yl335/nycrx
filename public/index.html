Prescription Access is a Critical Public Health Issue

NYCRx is a non-profit organizations that works to make prescription medicine accessible to under-served patients.

Maximizing 340B Savings

NYCRx works with 340B entities to maximize both their revenue and the savings to their patients.

Partnering with 19 Health Centers
Used on 238,000 Prescriptions
Over $2,500,000 Saved

Direct Patient Benefits

NYCRx offers a free prescription discount card that works at over 90% of pharmacies nation-wide and offers up to 65% off on prescriptions.

Partnering with 95% of All Pharmacies
Used on 53,000 Prescriptions
Over $1,700,000 Saved

Collaborative Research

NYCRx collaborates with universities and local government to further recognition of the importance of prescription access.

Founded By

NYC Dept of Health and Hygiene in 2006,
NYCRx is a non-profit organization that works with health safety net providers to ensure access to affordable medications for the medically under-served using 340B.

Since Then

NYCRx now covers NY, NJ and Connecticut.
Our mission has expanded to offer discount prescription cards and conduct in-depth researches into prescription access issues.
